How To Play

Desktop: WASD or arrow keys to move. Space or Z to fire. Shift or X for bomb. ESC pauses.

Mobile: Drag anywhere on the playfield to move. Fire is automatic. Double-tap for bomb.

Your hitbox is smaller than your sprite. You can squeeze through bullets. Don't get hit anyway.

Engine modules are still being wired. This release boots and renders the title card. The full game arrives in subsequent steps.
